About Tallownara

What Is Tallow,
and Why It Works

Tallow is rendered fat from cattle, used for centuries as one of nature's most effective moisturizers. Its lipid profile closely mirrors the sebum our own skin produces — making it uniquely compatible with human skin at a cellular level.

At Tallownara, we slow-render our tallow from grass-fed, pasture-raised cows to preserve every beneficial fatty acid, fat-soluble vitamin (A, D, E, K), and CLA that makes this ingredient so remarkable.
